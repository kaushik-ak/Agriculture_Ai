📜 Government Agricultural Schemes

All central government schemes for Indian farmers

🌾 PM-KISAN Scheme

Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi — Direct income support for farmers

₹6,000
Annual Benefit
3
Instalments/Year
9.3 Cr
Beneficiaries

Eligibility Criteria

  • All landholding farmer families in India
  • Must have valid Aadhaar card
  • Bank account linked with Aadhaar (DBT enabled)
  • Land records must be updated in state revenue records
  • Excludes: Income tax payers, govt employees, pension >₹10,000/month

How to Apply

  • Visit pmkisan.gov.in or nearest Common Service Centre (CSC)
  • Register with Aadhaar, mobile number & land details
  • Submit land records from state portal
  • Money transferred directly to bank account

🧪 Soil Health Card Scheme

Free soil testing and crop-specific nutrient recommendations

What is Tested?

🌱 N-P-K (Macro Nutrients)
⚗️ S, Zn, Fe, Cu, Mn, B
🔬 Soil pH & EC
🌍 Organic Carbon

How to Get Your Soil Health Card

  • Visit nearest Krishi Vigyan Kendra (KVK) or Agriculture Department office
  • Carry land records and Aadhaar card
  • Collect soil sample from your field (0-15 cm depth)
  • Results delivered within 15 days — card valid for 3 years
  • Visit soilhealth.dac.gov.in to download your card
